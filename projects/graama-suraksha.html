Graama Suraksha AI

n8n WhatsApp API Google Sheets Gemini AI Groq AI

Overview

Graama Suraksha AI is a community-focused cybersecurity initiative aiming to protect users from modern digital scams. Deployed directly within WhatsApp, the primary communication channel for millions, the bot analyzes suspicious messages and links using advanced AI language models to classify them as spam or ham securely.

What It Does

  • > Acts as a WhatsApp-based scam detection assistant.
  • > Leverages hybrid AI classification for spam/ham detection.
  • > Provides a persistent community alert system logged via Google Sheets.
  • > Analyzes audio payloads and operates multilingually to serve diverse user bases.
