AI-Powered LinkedIn Post Generator

n8n LinkedIn API Google Drive Gemini AI

Overview

A highly customized automation pipeline designed to maintain professional social media presence with minimal friction. It pulls seed ideas from Google Sheets, expands upon them thoughtfully via AI, allows for manual refinement if necessary, and securely posts to LinkedIn using their authorized APIs.

What It Does

  • > Watches Google Sheets for new content topics or prompts.
  • > Generates engaging, professional content using tailored Gemini AI prompts.
  • > Allows iterative refinement of generated drafts.
  • > Posts the final reviewed content automatically to LinkedIn.
