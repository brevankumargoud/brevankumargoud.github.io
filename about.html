About Me

I see cybersecurity as more than just a field — it’s a battlefield. I’m constantly preparing myself as a cyber warrior, understanding how systems break so they can be built stronger.

My focus is on offensive security, where I actively explore how attackers think, operate, and exploit vulnerabilities. I’m particularly drawn to the intensity and problem-solving involved in breaking systems — not to misuse them, but to understand and secure them better.

I strongly believe that as technology evolves, security becomes non-negotiable. My goal is to be part of that critical layer — identifying weaknesses before they can be exploited in the real world.

I don’t hack to cause damage. I hack to prove what’s possible — and to ensure it gets fixed.
